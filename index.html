Our Story

Merport Bistro was born from a passion for bringing people together through exceptional food and genuine hospitality. Located in the heart of Freeport's One Town Center, we've become a beloved gathering place for locals and visitors alike.

Our menu showcases the freshest local ingredients, prepared with care and served in a warm, inviting atmosphere that captures the spirit of Grand Bahama. Whether you're joining us for a leisurely meal or grabbing a quick bite to go, we promise an experience that will keep you coming back.

At Merport Bistro, we're more than just a restaurant - we're a proud part of the Freeport community, committed to creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate our island home.
